Social Media Addiction & How to Overcome It

A proposed screen time management app designed to help teenagers and parents track usage, set limits, build healthier habits, and reduce harmful social media overuse.

View Our Solution

The Problem

Social media has become a major part of teenage life, but many platforms are designed to keep users engaged for long periods of time through personalized algorithms. This can lead to unhealthy comparison, poor sleep, reduced attention span, anxiety, depression, and lower academic performance.

Mental Health

Excessive use may contribute to anxiety, depression, and negative self-comparison.

Sleep

Late-night scrolling can disrupt sleep routines and reduce sleep quality.

Productivity

Constant notifications and scrolling can make it harder for students to focus.

Proposed Solution

Our proposed solution is a mobile application that helps teenagers monitor and manage their social media use. The app tracks screen time, analyzes behavior patterns, sends reminders, creates limits, and rewards users for healthier decisions.

Technical Specifications

The application would be developed for both iOS and Android. A framework such as React Native could be used for the front end, while Firebase or Node.js could support the back end. The app would include user authentication, screen tracking integration, notification systems, data analytics, and privacy protections.

Implementation Timeline

Phase 1: Research

2 weeks — Analyze user behavior and review existing research.

Phase 2: Design

2 weeks — Create the user interface and app structure.

Phase 3: Development

4–6 weeks — Build tracking, limits, reminders, and rewards.

Phase 4: Testing

2 weeks — Beta test with students and fix issues.

Phase 5: Deployment

1 week — Launch the app and collect user feedback.

Feasibility & Limitations

This solution is feasible because the technology needed for screen time tracking, notifications, and app development already exists. However, limitations include users ignoring notifications, disabling restrictions, or having privacy concerns. Because of this, the app must be easy to use, secure, and motivating.
